What is the LGBT+ Community?

The LGBT+ Community is a community that either supports or are lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, and people along those lines.

What do those mean?

Lesbian-Females being attracted to other females

Gay-Males being attracted to males

Bisexual-Either gender being attracted to males and females

Transgender-Changing to the opposite gender
