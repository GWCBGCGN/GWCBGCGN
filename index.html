Girls Who Code at the Boys and Girls Club of Greater Nashua



This year the kids learned how to make web pages using HTML and CSS. They were asked to pick a topic that they cared about and wanted to share with everyone. Please take some time to find out what kids in Nashua, NH care about, why they care about it, and what they think should/could be done to make it better.

  • Maria Campos - Nature
  • Andre Maben - Childhood Hunger
  • Analise Maya - Video Games
  • Jacelly Rodriguez - Bullying
